Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Standard ticket?

The Standard ticket includes access to all 3 conference days, 48 curated technical talks, 2 workshop sessions, networking lunch & coffee breaks, and a conference swag bag with digital recordings for 30 days.

Can I upgrade my ticket from Standard to Pro later?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket at any time before the event starts by logging into your account or contacting our support team.

Is there a group discount for teams?

Yes! Our TEAM package is designed for groups and offers full access for multiple team members at a discounted rate. Check the pricing section for more details.

What is the refund policy?

We offer full refunds up to 14 days before the event. Inside of 14 days, we can help transfer your ticket to a colleague or credit it toward next year's event.
